
FILET OF BEEF WITH MUSTARD CREAM SAUCE

Serves 6


6– 8 ounces filet mignon steaks (about 1 – ½ inches thick)

1/3 cup chopped shallots

1 cup white wine

3 cups chicken stock 

1 1/2 cups heavy cream

1/4 cup Dijon mustard

2 tablespoons whole grain mustard

1.	Melt butter in large skillet over medium-high heat. Season steaks with salt and pepper. Cook to desired doneness, about 3 minutes per side for medium-rare. Transfer steaks to plate and set aside.

2.	Add in shallots to pan and cook until golden.  Add in white wine, scraping up all brown bits on bottom of pan.  Reduce wine by half, about 10 minutes.

3.	Add in heavy cream and reduce heat to low.  Continue to cook until reduced by half, about 5 minutes.  Strain sauce if desired.  

4.	Whisk in Dijon and whole grain mustard.  Season with salt and pepper.  Serve with whipped potatoes and spinach.
