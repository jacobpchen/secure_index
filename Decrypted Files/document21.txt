Filet of Beef with Madeira Sauce
Serves 4


2 pounds trimmed tenderloin, cut into 2 inch pieces
3 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons minced shallots
½ cup white wine
2 cups veal stock
1/3 cup dry Madeira


Heat the butter in a large skillet.  Add meat and cook until well browned. Remove meat and set aside.

Add shallots to pan and cook for a minute.  Add wine and let reduce to about 2 tablespoons.  Add in veal stock and reduce by ½.  Add Madeira and reduce by ½.
