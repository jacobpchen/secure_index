Seared Tuna Filet 

Serves 6


1/4 cup minced fresh ginger
5 scallions, green part only, chopped and divided
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup orange juice
Â¼ cup lite teriyaki sauce
3 tbs. lemon juice
3 dashes Tabasco sauce
6 (6-8 ounce) tuna steaks, about 1 1/2 - 2 inches thick
salt and pepper
1 tablespoon olive oil


1.	For the tuna: in a large bowl combine the ginger, 3 chopped scallions, garlic, orange juice, teriyaki sauce, lemon juice and Tabasco; stir well.  
2.	Add the tuna to bowl and let marinate 30 minutes, turning at least once. Remove the tuna from the marinade. 
3.	Strain marinade into a small saucepan and bring to a boil over high heat. Boil until slightly thickened, about 4-5 minutes; reserve. 
4.	Season the tuna with salt and pepper. In a large skillet over medium high heat, heat the oil. 
5.	Add the tuna and cook 3-4 minutes per side for rare. Serve tuna over warm lentil salad. 
6.	Spoon reduced marinade over tuna steaks and sprinkle with remaining scallions.
