Taiwanese Fried Chicken
Ingredients
Chicken (1lb)
Egg (1)
Thai Basil
Soy Paste (1.5 Tbl)
Shaoxing Wine (1/2 tbl)
Water (1/4 cup)
White pepper (1 tsp)
Five spice (1 tsp)
Sweet potato starch

Spices
Salt (1 tsp)
White Pepper (1 tsp)
Cayenne Pepper (1 tsp)
Sichuan Pepper (1tsp)
Marinade for 24 hours
Bread the chicken with sweet potato starch
Fry the chicken on medium heat for 4-5 minutes and take out as needed until all the chicken is done
Add basil
