Basmati Rice
Monday, March 2, 2020
4:05 PM
•	Soak one cup of basmati rice in cold water for 10 minutes 
•	drain the water and let the rice air dry for 10 min
•	Add oil to a stock pot and put rice in there and toast the rice for 1-2 minutes
•	Add 1.25 cups of water bring to boil
•	Lower heat to low and simmer for 17 minutes. 
•	After the 17 minutes let it cool for 10 minutes. **** DO NOT OPEN*** Until the 10 minutes have passed. (Put on back burner)
