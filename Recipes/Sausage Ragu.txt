Sausage Ragu
Saturday, January 18, 2020
11:28 AM
2 packs cherry tomatoes
1lb sausage
Pre-made red sauce
Kale
 
Set oven to 450
On stove top burst cherry tomatoes over high heat. Put sausage meat on top (add butter)
Put in oven to caramelize more
 
Once tomatoes are busted add the premade red sauce. Only enough to make it a sauce
Add kale
Toss back in oven until kale cooks through
