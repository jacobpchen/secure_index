Sauteed Spinach

Serves 6

2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
3 bunches spinach, about 3 pounds, thick stems removed, washed and left damp
salt and pepper

1. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add the garlic and cook until lightly golden, about 2 minutes. Add spinach, in bunches, adding more  to the skillet as it wilts and cook 2-3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
