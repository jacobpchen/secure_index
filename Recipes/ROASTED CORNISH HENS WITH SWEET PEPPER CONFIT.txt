ROASTED CORNISH HENS WITH SWEET PEPPER CONFIT

Serves 6


¼ cup chopped mixed fresh herbs (such as thyme, rosemary or parsley)

3 shallots, minced

3 large Cornish game hen, halved lengthwise (about 1 1/2 pounds) 

3 tablespoons butter, room temperature

Preheat oven to 450°F. 
1.	Mix herbs and shallots in small bowl. Slide fingertips under skin of hen halves to loosen. Spread about 2 tablespoons of herb mixture under skin of each hen half. Rub skin with butter; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place hen halves in shallow roasting pan. 
2.	Roast hen halves until golden and cooked through, about 30 minutes. 
3.	Serve with sweet pepper confit.



SWEET PEPPER CONFIT

2 Tablespoons olive oil

1 red pepper, cut into ¼ inch dice

1 yellow pepper, cut into ¼ inch dice

1 shallot, minced

1 garlic clove, minced

1 teaspoon fresh thyme

1.	Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and cook over medium until very soft and tender, about 30-40 minutes.
2.	Remove from heat and serve with roasted Cornish hen.
