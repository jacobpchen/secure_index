Maple Roasted Root Vegetables

Serves 6

12 shallots, peeled
4 parsnips, peeled and cut diagonally into 2-inch pieces
4 medium white turnips, peeled and cut into sixths
2 Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into sixths
1 large sweet potato, peeled and cut into 2-inch long pieces
2 carrots, peeled and cut diagonally into 2-inch pieces
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons maple syrup
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon fresh ground nutmeg
salt and pepper

1. Preheat oven to 425F. In a large bowl, combine the shallots, parsnips, turnips, Yukon gold potatoes, sweet potato, carrots, olive oil, maple syrup, cinnamon and nutmeg. Season with salt and pepper. Spread vegetables on a large baking sheet in a single layer. Roast until tender and golden brown, stirring occasionally, 55-65 minutes.
