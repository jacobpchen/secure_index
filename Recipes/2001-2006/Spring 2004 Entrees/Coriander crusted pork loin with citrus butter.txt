CORIANDER CRUSTED PORK LOIN WITH CITRUS BUTTER

Serves 6


3 ½ pounds pork loin, trimmed of fat

2 tablespoons crushed coriander

1 tablespoon crushed black pepper

1 tablespoon crushed cumin seeds

1 teaspoon hot paprika

¼ cup plain yogurt

Preheat oven to 425◦F.

1.	Combine coriander, black pepper, cumin, paprika and yogurt.  ]

2.	Spread it on to pork loin and allow to marinate ½ hour at least.

3.	Place in a roasting pan and roast until instant read thermometer registers 160◦F.  Remove from oven, allow to rest 10 minutes, slice thinly and serve with citrus glaze, barley salad and sautéed swiss chard.

CITRUS GLAZE

1 tablespoons olive oil

1 onion, minced

½ cup orange juice

2 tablespoons Dijon mustard

2 tablespoons honey

¼ cup thinly sliced scallions

1 tablespoon lemon juice

2 tablespoons butter

1.	Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium high heat.  Add in onion and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.  

2.	Add in orange juice, Dijon and honey and reduce by half.  Remove from heat and whisk in scallions, lemon juice and butter.

3.	Serve drizzled over pork.
