PAN SEARED TUNA WITH GINGER SHIITAKE SAUCE

Serves 6


6- 6 ounce tuna steaks, about 1 ½ inch thick

2 tablespoon peanut oil

3 tablespoon butter

2 tablespoons finely chopped ginger

4 cloves garlic, minced

8 ounces fresh shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and caps thinly sliced

¼ cup soy sauce

¼ cup vegetable stock

1/3 cup thinly sliced scallions

¼ cup heavy cream

2 tablespoons fresh lime juice



1.	Heat peanut oil in a large sauté pan until hot.  Add 3 pieces tuna and cook about 1-2 minutes.  Turn tuna over and cook another 1-2 minutes.  Transfer tuna to a platter and set aside. Repeat with over 3 pieces of tuna.

2.	Place butter in same sauté pan.  Add in ginger and garlic and sauté about 30 seconds.  


3.	Add in shiitakes, soy sauce and vegetable stock and simmer for about 5 minutes, until shitakes are slightly softened.


4.	Add in scallions and heavy cream and continue to cook another 2-3 minutes until cream slightly thickened.  Remove from heat and stir in lime juice.  Place sauce on top of seared tuna and serve with rice and spring vegetable succotash.
