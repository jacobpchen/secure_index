Carbonara
Monday, February 24, 2020
3:41 PM
Cook pasta and reserve a cup of pasta water
Cook the pancetta and render the fat
Add fresh cracked pepper to the pancetta to toast the pepper
 
Put the pasta in a large mixing bowl
Add half a cup of reserve pasta water
Add the pancetta and fat
Add a mountain of cheese to protect from the heat
Drop two large egg yolks onto the top of the cheese
Use tongs and mix quickly to get a silky sauce
