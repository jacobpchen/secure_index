Buttermilk Mashed Yukon Gold Potatoes

Serves 6

2 1/2 pounds Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 2 inch pieces
8 tablespoons butter
1 cup low fat buttermilk, at room temperature
1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
salt and pepper

1. In a large pot over medium-high heat, combine the potatoes with cold water to cover. Bring to a boil; cook until tender 22-28 minutes. Drain potatoes and reserve in colander. Return pot to the stove over medium heat and melt butter. Remove pot from the heat and add the potatoes and buttermilk. Mash the potatoes with a wire potato masher until fairly smooth. Stir in the chives and season with salt and pepper.
