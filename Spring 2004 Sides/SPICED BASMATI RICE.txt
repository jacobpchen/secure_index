SPICED BASMATI RICE

Serves 6


2 tablespoons oil

½ cup finely chopped onion

2 cups basmati rice

1 cinnamon stick

2 large strips of lemon zest (about 2 X 2 inches)

3 ½ cups vegetable or chicken stock

2 tablespoons butter, optional

2 tablespoons flat leaf parsley

1.	Heat oil in a 4 quart saucepan over medium heat.  Add onion and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.  

2.	Stir in rice and cook another 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  

3.	Add in cinnamon stick, lemon zest and stock.  Bring to a boil, stir rice, cover pot tightly and cook over low heat about 20-25 minutes, until rice is cooked through.  Remove zest and cinnamon.

4.	Fluff with fork, adding in butter and parsley.
