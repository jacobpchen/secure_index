ROASTED FINGERLING POTATOES

Serves 6


3 pounds fingerling potatoes, quartered

¼ cup olive oil

¼ cup chopped parsley

Preheat oven to 450◦F.
 
1.	Toss potatoes and olive oil together.  Season with salt and pepper.  

2.	Roast potatoes until cooked through, about ½ hour.  

3.	Remove from oven, toss with parsley.  Serve with Chicken breasts and tomato salad.
