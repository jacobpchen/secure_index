SAUTÈED SWISS CHARD AND SHALLOTS

Serves 6



3 ½ pounds Swiss chard

¼ cup olive oil

12 shallots, peeled and quartered

2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

1.	Cut stems and center ribs away from Swiss Chard leaves Chop stems and ribs thinly and set aside.  Chop leaves coarsely and set aside separately.  

2.	In a large pot heat oil over medium heat.  Add in stems, ribs and shallots and cook about 5 minutes, until slightly tender.  

3.	Add in leaves and cooking, turning frequently, until wilted.  

4.	Add in balsamic and season with salt and pepper.
