ROASTED ASPARAGUS WITH LEMON BUTTER

Serves 6

3 pounds fresh asparagus (36 medium), trimmed and peeled
3 tablespoon oil
 
Preheat oven to 500°F. 
1.	In a large shallow baking pan toss asparagus with oil and salt and pepper to taste until coated well.
2.	Roast asparagus, shaking pan occasionally, until tender and lightly browned, about 8 minutes. 
3.	Remove pan from oven and toss with lemon butter.  
LEMON BUTTER
½ - 1 stick butter
juice of 1 lemon
zest of 2 lemons
1.  Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
